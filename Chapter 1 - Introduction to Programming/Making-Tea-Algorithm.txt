**MAKING TEA ALGORITHM**

STEP 1:	Start
STEP 2:	Put water in the kettle.
STEP 3:	Switch the power on for the kettle.
STEP 4:	Place the tea bag in the cup.
STEP 5:	Fill the cup with the boiled water.
STEP 6:	Pour milk into the cup.
STEP 7:	Pour sugar into the cup
STEP 8:	Stir with spoon.
STEP 9:	End.
